Used to transform data before displaying it on in the browser
pipes take data as input and format or transform the data to some form

syntax: Expression| pipename[:parameters]
eg:- 'angular' | Uppercase o/p-> ANGULAR 
Built in Pipes:
CurrencyPipe, DataPipe, DEcimalPipe, JsonPipe,LowerCasePipe, UpperCasePipe,PercentPipe,SilencePipe,AsyncPipe

Custom pipes:-
Syntax: ng g p pipe-name or ng generate pipe pipe-name
